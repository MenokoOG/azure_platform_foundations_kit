# 09 — Governance: Policy, Locks, Management Groups

## Azure Policy (guardrails)
Examples:
- require tags
- restrict regions
- restrict public IPs in prod
- enforce approved SKUs

## Resource Locks
- CanNotDelete: prevents deletion
- ReadOnly: prevents modification

## Management Groups
Apply governance across subscriptions.

## Enterprise hygiene
- naming + tags enforced
- region restrictions
- logging standardized
- public access minimized
